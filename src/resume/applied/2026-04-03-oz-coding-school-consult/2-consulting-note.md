## 찾아볼 키워드

### xyz formula

- "Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y] by doing [Z]"
    - X (What was accomplished): The positive outcome, result, or improvement.
    - Y (How it was measured): The quantifiable metric (e.g., percentages, dollar amounts, time saved, number of users).
    - Z (How it was achieved): The specific actions, skills, or tools used.
- Examples of the XYZ Method
    - Before: Managed social media accounts.
    - After: (X) Developed new strategy, (Y) increasing followers by 30% and website traffic by 15%, (Z) by implementing
      a targeted content calendar.
    - Before: Improved customer service.
    - After: (X) Reduced customer complaints by 60% (Y) by (Z) redesigning the support ticket workflow and training a
      team of 12.

### 전체를 뒷받침하는 프로젝트:

- 고객으로부터 시작해야. 고객이 원하는 게 뭐였는지.
- 고객의 요구가 flow의 시작 (문제를 정의해야 한다)
